Rainforest Adventure

Rainforest cover upto 6% of the Earth's land. The forest floor recieves only 2% of sunlight. The roots of some trees can grow naturally together to from strong natural bridges. There are trees which seem to be walking through the forest. It grows new roots in one direction while the other roots die. Over many years, the trees would have moved away from their original places. There are some fascinating birds that live these rainforests. Cassowry is a bird which is 6 feet tall. Even though it cannot fly, it runs at a speed of 50 kmph and can jump to a height of 5 feet. Another unusual animal is the Okapi. The Okapi is a weird cross of a giraffe and zebra. Flying dragons are lizards which can glide from tree to tree. It can glide upto 100 feet in a single jump.

Jungle River Journey

A river is a natural stream of flowing water. Even though they constitute less than 1% of the world's freshwater, they provide water for almost all living beings. The rivers can have multiple colors such as black, brown, green, blue and crystal clear. There are many amazing vegetation that grow in these rivers. Leaves of the Giant Water Lily can grow upto a length of 3 meters. On blooming, they are white on the first day and pink on the second. Water Soldier has a very unique characteristic. It lives under water during winter and comes to the surface in summer to bloom. There are many animals that live in these waters. Pink River Dolphins can grow upto a length of 2.5 meters. They can turn their heads 90° which helps them swim through flooded forests. The Black Caiman is one of the largest crocdilian species. It can grow over 5 meters in length. Arapaima is one of the largest freshwater fish in the world. It can grow over 3 meters long. Piranha is one of the most dangerous fishin the world. It is found in the Amozon river. They are famous for their sharp teeth and powerful bite. Most Piranhas grow upto a length of 15 - 30 centimeters but some species can grow upto a lenght of 50 centimeters.

Night Jungle Safari

As the sun sets, the jungle transforms into a mysterious world. On a night jungle safari, visitors may spot glowing eyes of nocturnal animals, hear the calls of owls, frogs, and insects, and watch bats flying silently through the trees. Fireflies light up the forest like tiny stars, while some fungi glow naturally in the darkness. Along rivers, moonlight reflects on the water as fish, turtles, and other creatures become active. Every sound, shadow, and movement reveals the hidden beauty of the jungle, making a night safari an unforgettable adventure into nature's secret world.
