Amazing Sea Animals

Orca (Killer Whale)

They are the largest members of the dolphin family. They can swim at speeds of about 56 kmph. Orcas live in groups called pods. Each pod has a different way of communicating.

Manta Ray

Manta Ray has the largest brain which makes highly intelligent. Its wingspan can reach upto 7 meters. Each manta ray has a unique pattern of spots on its belly, like a human fingerprint.

Great Hammerhead Shark

It can grow upto a length of 6 meters and weighs about 450 kg. They have a 360 degree view of their surroundings. They can reach a maximum speed of 40 kmph. They can detect the heartbeat of the animals hidden under the sand.
